Llamatik Code – Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16th, 2026

Llamatik AI ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Llamatik Code ("Software") handles information when you use our IntelliJ plugin and related services.

1. Overview

Llamatik Code is designed as a local-first AI tool.

By default:

  • Your source code is processed locally on your device
  • Your data is not transmitted to our servers
  • We do not collect or store your code

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Data Collected by Default

We do not collect personal data or source code by default when you use the Software.

2.2 Optional Data (If Enabled)

Some features may involve external services or future functionality (such as licensing or updates). In those cases, we may process limited data such as:

  • License or subscription status
  • Basic technical information (e.g., plugin version, OS)
  • Anonymous usage metrics (only if explicitly enabled)

We will clearly indicate when such features are active.

2.3 Third-Party Services

If you configure the Software to use third-party services (e.g., external AI models or APIs):

  • Your data may be sent to those services
  • Their privacy policies will apply

You are responsible for reviewing and accepting those third-party terms.

3. Local Processing

Llamatik Code primarily runs AI models locally on your device.

This means:

  • Your code remains under your control
  • No external transmission occurs unless explicitly configured

4. Data Storage

  • Chats and project-related data are stored locally on your device
  • We do not have access to this data

You are responsible for managing and securing your local environment.

5. Security

We design our Software to minimize data exposure by keeping processing local.

However, no system is completely secure. You are responsible for:

  • Securing your device
  • Managing access to your development environment

6. Children's Privacy

The Software is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13 (or the minimum legal age in your jurisdiction).

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

We will update the "Last updated" date accordingly. Continued use of the Software constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
